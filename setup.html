Setting Up Ad Bot

By following this video or these instructions below, you can set up Ad Bot in just seconds!

1. Invite Ad-Bot to your server.

2. Create a channel called ads.

3. Type: ^custom^ad [Description of your server here]

4. Type: ^test to make sure everything is working.

5. Type: ^ad every now and then for your server to be advertised on other servers.

Thats it! Your all set!
