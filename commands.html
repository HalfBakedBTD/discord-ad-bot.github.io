Ad Bot Commands

Ad-Bot mainly has commands for advertising your server. Prefix: ^
Command What they do..
help Sends a direct message with all the bot commands.
test Tests to see if the bot is properly set up.
info Shows how many online users, and server the bot is on.
ad Bumps your server to the top of the list, and advertises it. (can be used every 10 seconds)
custom^ad Lets you make your own message when advertising your server.
big-ad-button Can be pressed every 10 minutes. Advertises your server so it stays on top for 10 minutes.
